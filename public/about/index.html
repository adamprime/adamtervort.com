  • I lived in Taiwan for 10 years
  • I’m fluent in Mandarin
  • My first “real job” way playing jazz bass around Kansas City
  • My second “real job” was teaching ESL, both in the US and in Asia

One of the things I most enjoy is sharing what I know. If there’s something I can help you with please let me know! I have a number of courses available on my side project, Hammock Marketing.
